# External Homepage Copy Angles

Use these lines for public-facing pages.

Rules:
- short sentences
- literal language
- no legal threats
- no accusations

## Primary angle

Private, factual building issue tracking for residents.

## Supporting lines

- Coordinate safely with neighbors.
- Add “me too” reports without long writing.
- Keep evidence private by default.
- Generate neutral notices in standard or very simple English.
- Export a clear timeline for inspectors or legal-aid workflows.

## Competitive angle variants

- Built for whole-building patterns, not one-off forms.
- Resident-controlled records with privacy-first defaults.
- One structured timeline from report to notice to export.

## Compliance reminders

Do not publish wording that implies:
- guaranteed legal outcomes
- legal advice
- public shaming or threats
