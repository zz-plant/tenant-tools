# Competitor Battlecard (Internal)

This document is for internal strategy and messaging use.

Audience:
- product
- stewardship
- partnerships

Do not publish this table as-is on public pages.

## Purpose

Map nearby alternatives to Building Ledger and clarify where our fit is strongest.

## Battlecard table

| Segment | Typical buyer | Strongest claim | Weakest point vs Building Ledger | Suggested homepage copy angle |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tenant-rights workflow tools (JustFix-style) | Individual tenants and legal-aid partners | Fast legal-process workflows and letter support | Usually optimized for individual case flow, not building-level “me too” aggregation and resident-only coordination | Built for whole-building patterns, not one-off forms. |
| City 311 and civic complaint systems | Residents reporting to city agencies | Official city intake and inspection channel | Not designed for resident-private coordination, private evidence defaults, or reusable neutral notice drafting | Track issues as neighbors first, then use 311 when it is the next normal step. |
| Property-management maintenance portals | Landlords and property managers | Fast maintenance ticket routing and status tracking | Control usually sits with management. Resident anti-retaliation defaults are weaker. | Resident-controlled records with privacy-first defaults. |
| Civic/public issue reporting apps | Cities, neighborhood groups, public reporters | Public visibility can pressure response | Public visibility can increase retaliation and doxxing risk in tenant disputes | Private by default. Safer for real tenant risk. |
| Generic tool stacks (forms + sheets + chat) | Tenant organizers and informal building groups | Free and flexible with tools people already know | Fragmented flow. Hard to keep one factual timeline from report to notice to export. | One structured timeline from report to notice to export. |

## Notes for product and copy reviews

- Keep claims factual and simple.
- Keep language neutral and non-idiomatic.
- Do not claim legal outcomes.
- Do not claim this replaces legal aid.
